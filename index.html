About Me

Tiye Sheppard is a multimedia storyteller and current student at CUNY Graduate School of Journalism in New York City. She specializes in video, audio and interactive media to share underrepresented voices.

She completed her undergraduate education in at the University of San Francisco with a bacherlor's degree in Media Studies and Film Studies. She also minored in Latin American Studies, which shows in her dedication to bilingual stories in Spanish and English.

After moving to New York City in 2016, she now spends her time freelancing, exploring and meeting new people

She is a member of the National Association of Black Journalists and serves as Treasurer of the CUNY Association of Black Journalists.
